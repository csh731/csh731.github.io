my greatest skill

I am a girl who loves sports very much,so I almost exercise every day.As a result,my greatest skill is doing sports.
First of all, I am so good at running that I can always get medals in the school sports meetings.I have had three gold medals in middle schools, and I came first in the 4*100 meter relay race this year. I worked as a leader in our team,and we practised hard every PE class. After victory, I not only felt a sense of achievement, but also realize the importance better of working as a team.
Secondly, I can also do well in playing football.I learned playing football in Grade 3 when I was a primary school student. I practised dribbling,defending and shooting in every football class. Moreover,I also did physical training for 1 hour after school every day. As the captain of our football team, I led our team to win the second place in the district competition excitedly.
Thirdly, I am able to play volleyball well too. Although I first learned it at Grade 7 in my junior school, I am honored to join the school team and succeeded in coming first as the main player in the school volleyball competition. As a team match, we players and our PE teacher were united as one. We always listened the teacher carefully in order to do the standard movements well.We would do the same movements in many times and nobody complained. Since all of us had known how strong our rivals were, everyone practised even harder than any of our rival. Although we got injured often,it didn’t stop us from being competitive. Instead, we practised even harder than before.At last, we gained the first successfully. I thought the success was all due to the effort we had put in.
In conclusion, I think doing sports is not only my greatest skill,but also help me adjust my mood whenever I feel tired after studying. In my opinion,doing sports is my best helper!
