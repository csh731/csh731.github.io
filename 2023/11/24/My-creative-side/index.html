My creative side

First of all, I am an original and innovative thinker and I can often use this ability when I solve problems around me like solving Maths problems in my study.

When I was in kindergarten, I was interested in playing with and kneading clay.I could use my imagination to make all kinds of objects,including animals,cars,cartoon characters and so on. They filled my childhood with laughter and a sense of achievement in my creativity.

As time went by, Lego deeply attracted me both when I was at primary school as a pupil and as a middle school student studying at junior high school. After I finished my studies, whenever I had free time, I would always use my imagination to make creations without reading instruction books. I went to Lego shops to buy some parts from time to time, and then put them into various models. It was great fun! As the saying goes , “Donot burn the candle at both ends.”It could not only relax me after a busy day of studying, but also improve my imagination.

I use my imagination in my studies as well, especially in my Mathematics study. When I solve problems in this subject, I always try my best to work them out in as many ways as possible. After I developed this fantastic habit , I always try to problem solve being methodical. I can solve the problem one way and verify it another way to reduce the times of errors. For example,when I solved a quadratic equation,I could calculate the number of focal points on the X-axis by arithmetic, and at the same time I could check the number of points of intersection by drawing.Moreover, I can deepen my knowledge, and often take high marks in the exams often.

In conclusion, I agree a lot that imagination and creativity are very important both in my studies and in my life I cannot live without them! I will use them more in the future and I believe I can make great progress both in my studies and my life. I hope I will get into my dream university with this good skill in several years.

Chen Sihan
